Goodbye Surviving, Hello Thriving

Google has been investing in AI for many years and is bringing its benefits to individuals, businesses, and communities. Whether it’s publishing state-of-the-art research, building helpful products, or developing tools and resources that enable others, we remain committed to making AI accessible to everyone.

“People worry that computers will get too smart and take over the world, but the real problem is that they’re too stupid and they’ve already taken over the world.”

– Pedro Domingos

Much of this progress has historically depended on supervised learning, where AI systems learn from labeled examples. Now, thanks to advances in generative AI, we’re entering a new era of AI that’s unlocking entirely new ways of engaging with information. These models are capable of generating text, images, and other content, opening doors to creativity and productivity at a scale never before possible.

We’re so excited by the potential of generative AI. And its applications are just unfolding — from helping people express themselves creatively, to solving everyday tasks faster, to new forms of applications, to transforming how businesses and governments engage their audiences and constituents. Stay tuned for more to come as this next evolution unfolds ahead.
